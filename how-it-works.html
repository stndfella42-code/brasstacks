How It Works

From discovery to launch in 3-4 weeks

1

Phase 1: Discovery Week 1

  • Initial consultation call
  • Understand your business, current system, pain points
  • Define scope (website only, POS integration, or full package)
2

Phase 2: Design & Planning Week 1-2

  • Design your custom website based on your brand
  • Map out POS integration points
  • Show you mockups for approval
  • Incorporate your feedback
3

Phase 3: Development Week 2-3

  • Build your website with menu integration and ordering flow
  • Integrate with your POS system (API setup, real-time data)
  • Connect to your database for live menu/inventory updates
  • Implement language support if needed
4

Phase 4: Testing & Training Week 3-4

  • Full end-to-end testing (orders flow correctly to POS)
  • Staff training on using the new system
  • Final adjustments based on your feedback
5

Phase 5: Launch Week 4

  • Deploy to production and go live
  • First week support included

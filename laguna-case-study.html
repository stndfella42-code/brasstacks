Laguna Project Breakdown

A complete operational overhaul combining a custom website with a native Point-of-Sale ecosystem.

1

Frictionless Online Ordering

We eliminated the bottleneck of waiting for a server during rush periods.

  • Mobile-First Native Browsing: Customers scan a QR code at their table to instantly pull up the live menu on their phones.
  • Direct-to-Kitchen: Orders placed on the phone bypass the standard server ticketing process, going straight to the prep line.
  • Zero App Install: A lightning-fast web experience that feels like a native app without forcing customers to download anything.

Customer-Facing Mobile Interface

2

Complete Admin Control

The Laguna POS system isn't just a cash register—it's the brain of the business.

  • Centralized Inventory: Track every ingredient in real-time. Receive automated alerts when stock drops below critical thresholds.
  • Live Menu Syncing: Need to 86 an item? Mark it out of stock in the POS, and it instantly disappears from the customer-facing website—no double entry required.
  • Dynamic Pricing Rules: Toggle daily specials, happy hour pricing, and dynamic margins globally with a single click.

Centralized Admin Control Panel

3

Kitchen & Bar Synchronization

Routing systems that prevent lost tickets and miscommunications.

  • Intelligent Ticket Routing: Beverage orders automatically print at the bar, while hot food tickets route instantly to the Kitchen Display System (KDS).
  • Order State Tracking: Waitstaff can view at a glance whether an order is "In Queue", "Prepping", or "Ready for Runner".
  • Accelerated Turnaround: By tightly coupling the ordering system to the prep stations, Laguna shaved crucial minutes off table turnaround times.
